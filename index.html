About

In today's digital world, social platforms like Telegram offer a unique opportunity for developers to create interactive and engaging experiences. The Lucky Wheel Telegram Bot aims to leverage this by offering users a chance to test their luck and win prizes through a simple and enjoyable game.

ROADMAP

Phase 1

  • Prize Management: Develop a prize management system. Implement different types of prizes and rarity levels. Allow users to view their prize history.

Phase 2

  • Beta Testing and Feedback: Launch a closed beta version for a limited user group. Gather user feedback and bug reports. Iterate and make improvements based on user feedback.
  • Public Launch: Launch the Lucky Wheel Telegram Bot to the public. Implement user acquisition strategies, including marketing and promotion. Monitor server performance and scalability.

Phase 3

  • Monetization Strategies Implement monetization features, such as: Advertisement placements. Premium memberships with exclusive benefits. Sponsored prizes and partnerships.
  • Community Building Build and nurture an active user community. Engage with users on social media and Telegram channels. Collect user-generated content and testimonials.

HOW TO PLAY

STEP 1

Create an Account on Lucky Wheel Bot

First, interact with our bot to set up your account and fund wallet

STEP 2

Deposit $LWB Tokens

To start betting, you'll need to deposit $LUCKYWHEELBOT tokens. Use the bot's generated wallet to make your deposit.

STEP 3

Initiate a Game Round

Initiate a game round by clicking on the "Get Lucky!" button on your mini dashboard. This starts a new round of games. Remember, only one round can be active at any given time

STEP 4

Place Your Bet

Use the /bet command followed by your chosen option and the amount you wish to bet.
Syntax: /bet [option] [amount] Example: /bet red 50

STEP 5

Explore Various Betting Options

Black: 2x Payout
Red: 2x Payout
Green: 18x Payout
Low (1-18): 2x Payout
Numbers (0, 00, 1-36): 36x Payout
Even: 2x Payout
Odd: 2x Payout
High (19-36): 2x Payout

STEP 6

Withdraw Your Winnings

Once you've accumulated some winnings, you can withdraw them from the bot.
